What We Do?

The Encyclopedia for Mythological Creatures is a site with the goal to inform you
on a few different mythologies and their gods, goddesses, angels, monsters and demons from
around the world. We strive to educate you on the mythological worlds that surround us
and we hope that you will take away at least one piece of magical information along with
a little fairy friend that will sit on your shoulder and tell you stories of the worlds
she has walked through.
